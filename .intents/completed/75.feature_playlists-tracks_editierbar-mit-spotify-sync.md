### 3.1 Implementation Context

* **`SpotifyPlaylistsGateway`** (`app/services/spotify_playlists_gateway.rb`) bekommt drei neue
  Low-Level-Schreibmethoden, analog zu den bestehenden Lese-Methoden (`find`, `tracks_for`):

    `change_details!(name:)`, gibt die neue `snapshot_id` zurĂĽck.
  - `add_track(playlist, track)` â†’ `add_tracks!([...])`, gibt neue `snapshot_id` zurĂĽck.

* **Neuer Service `PlaylistSpotifyWriteService`** (`app/services/playlist_spotify_write_service.rb`)

    nachgezogen werden wie beim Sync, siehe `BuildMusicNetService#assign_libraries`).
  - `#add_track!(playlist, track)` - pusht (falls `spotify_id` vorhanden), dann
    `PlaylistTrack.create!(playlist:, track:, added_at: Time.current)` +
